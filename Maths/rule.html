Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Rules

Step 1: Look at the output numbers (y). How much does it change each time? This tells you the step (multiplier).

Step 2: Look at the input numbers (x). How much does x change each time? Divide the change in y by the change in x to find the multiplier.

Step 3: Multiply x by the multiplier. Check how far it is from the first output. Add that difference to make the rule start correctly.
